ABOUT

SASE NORTHEAST REGIONAL CONFERENCE

The annual Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE) Northeast Regional Conference (NERC) on February 22, 2020 at the Marriott Boston Quincy Hotel is the largest SASE conference in the Northeast Region bringing together more than 30 collegiate chapters. At NERC, you can expect to hear from inspiring speakers, attend helpful workshops, connect with dozens of prestigious companies, and meet up with your one and only SASE fam! This year's theme "Expanding Horizons" challenges you to expand your horizons as we learn how to explore, excel, and empower in everything we do. Hope to see you there!

  • We encourage our attendees to think with an open mind and to broaden their horizons. Navigating the uncharted waters the future holds can be quite challenging at times, and that’s why we want to inspire you to continue to move forward with confidence. We hope that with these workshops, you can gain a new perspective on your desired career path or at the least, change how you think about life post-college. Regardless of the outcome, you will obtain useful tips that should help you find success in life.

  • We want to encourage our attendees to break the status quo and not settle on just being average. With these workshops, you should be able to obtain tips/advice from those who are experienced, which can help you carve out your future and guide you on your way to success.

  • Not only do we want to help our attendees find their own success, we also want to help them become an inspiration to others. Learn how to foster the next generation of leaders, and in the process of growing, inspire others to improve and flourish.

MARRIOTT QUINCY BOSTON HOTEL

Located on Massachusetts' South Shore, the hotel is just a short walk or complimentary shuttle ride away from the Quincy Adams T Station, providing direct access to downtown Boston; nearby suburbs including Weymouth and Cohasset are close by as well. It offers contemporary rooms, work desks with ergonomic chairs, high-speed Wi-Fi and deluxe bath toiletries. When you come to NERC, book your stay here!

For GPS directions use 1200 Crown Colony Drive, Quincy MA 02169 as hotel address.

PARKING

Parking is available at the hotel. For those who would like to park their vehicles on-site at the hotel, please be aware of the $13 daily fee.

POLICY

SPONSOR PAYMENT AND CANCELLATION

The deadline for payment of the fully completed form is 30 days prior to the event date. Payment in full for any sponsored items contained in this 2020 Sponsorship Packet must be received no later than 30 days prior to the regional conference date. Failure to comply will result in no access to the conference events or items.

SPONSOR REFUND POLICY

Within 30 days to event date, a NO REFUND policy will be introduced. A full invoice will be issued for all sponsored items prior to 30 days of the event date except registrations (credit card payment only).

ATTENDEE PAYMENT AND TRANSFER

After individual registration and payment for the event has been completed, a NO REFUND policy will be introduced. However, those registered may transfer registration to another person by contacting gr-rc@wpi.edu.
